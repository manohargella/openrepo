Contributors to OpenRepo

We are immensely grateful to our wonderful community of contributors who have made OpenRepo a thriving and valuable resource for learners worldwide. OpenRepo is a collaborative effort, and we welcome contributions from developers, educators, and anyone passionate about spreading knowledge.

Ways to Contribute

  • Submit new repositories: Help expand our collection by adding high-quality resources you've discovered.
  • Review and curate repositories: Participate in the curation process to ensure the quality and relevance of content.
  • Improve existing repositories: Contribute updates and improvements to existing resources.
  • Report issues: If you encounter any issues, bugs, or have suggestions, please let us know.
